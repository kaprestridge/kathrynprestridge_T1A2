Hello

Hi, I’m Kat. I am currently studying to become a Full Stack Developer with Coder Academy in Sydney. I am excited to be beginning a fun and exciting new career in tech. I live with a gorgeous Kelpie mix pup named Tess, three cats named River, Batman and Poe and a bearded dragon named Edward. They keep me on my toes! In my free time I play a huge range of tabletop games, RPGs and video games. I love movies and I’m somehow still not sick of the superhero genre. Podcasts are my happy place.

Work and Eduction

I have been a pet stylist for almost 9 years! In that time I have had opportunities to manage teams, train new people and build wonderful relationships with clients and their pets. I became a groomer whilst studying at Western Sydney University where I received a Bachelors of Science in Biology. During my degree I completed some elective subjects in computer science and fell in love with programming. I had hoped to pursue a career in Biology but have since decided to come back that love and work towards being a developer. I love animals and love managing my team but I am ready to step away from the pet industry after finding I have reached a place where I was no longer learning or being challenged.
